What is considered a successful ecommerce conversion rate?

+

A successful ecommerce conversion rate is percentages above 3%. Percentages between 2.5% and 3% are considered average ecommerce conversion rates.

What is a click-through rate (CTR)?

+

A click-through rate (CTR) is the percentage of people that click on an advert after seeing the advert.
