What do we offer?

SEO

We help you get to first position in the user’s list of search results. People will see you website before other websites. This will encourage more visitors to visit your website. We do this by building valuable backlinks and by writing content that includes relevant keywords. We also optimise your websites performance to ensure a pleasant experience for your users.

PPC

We encourage more users to visit your site by using Pay per Click (PPC) ads. These ads ensure that your website is first in the list of websites. You'll only have to pay for this service if a user clicks on your link and visits your website. This ensures that your money is not wasted.

Analytics

We analyse the data to build effective marketing strategies that suit your business. We look at various key performance indicators such as bounce rate, length of time spent on pages and cart abandonment rate. The tools we use for this are Google Analytics and SEO machine. We use these tools to ensure that we're improving the areas of your website that need to be improved.

Content marketing

We have a team of talented graphic designers, writers and film editors that work together to tell a story to interest your visitors. Our team have a huge success rate, increasing traffic to our websites by 90%. They can do the same for your website.

Web design

We can design and build a bespoke website that suits your specific needs. We have a team of frontend developers who can ensure you have an engaging and useful website. There's also a team of backend developers who ensure that data is being handled correctly.

Email marketing

Email marketing is a very effective tool to engage customers and potential customers. The user gets the chance to sign up for the email newsletter when they visit the website. The purpose of the newsletter isn't just to try to sell things to your users. It's also to provide them with useful and interesting information.
