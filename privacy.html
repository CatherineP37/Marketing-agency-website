Privacy policy

This privacy notice tells you want to expect when Digital Marketing collects your personal data when you engage with our services or members of staff. This privacy notice applies to information we collect about visitors to our website and customers who use our services. It also explains how we’ll store and handle that data, and keep it safe in accordance with the Data Protection Act (GDPR) and Privacy & Electronic Communications Regulation. We only collect personal data when you provide it to us.

We will only use data captured for specific purposes related to the provision of services by Digital Marketing. These purposes include responding to enquiries received through our contact form and social medial platforms. We may also use your information to keep you up to date with relevant services and useful updates. Recipients are given the option to request for their data to be removed from our records.

With all our clients we collect information (name, contact, details and card details for payment). We only publish client reviews with permission from the client.

We hold the personal information you provide for a maximum of 5 years after you final engagement with us. Once the five-year designated time period has elapsed the information is deleted.

Data protection rights

Under data protection law, you have rights including:

  • You have the right to ask us for copies of your personal information.
  • You have the right to ask us to rectify the information you think is inaccurate.
  • You have the right to ask us to delete the data we have about you in certain circumstances.
  • Your right to restrict the processing of your data - you have the right to object to the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.

Cookies

Security

We are committed to keeping your information secure and take appropriate steps to protect it.
